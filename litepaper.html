Executive Summary

Neural Nexus ($NAE) is an AI-driven crypto ecosystem built on the Base network, featuring a unique 25% ecosystem burn mechanism that creates permanent digital scarcity.

Core Innovation

  • Fixed Supply: 1,000,000,000 NAE (1 Billion)
  • Ecosystem Burn: 25% tax on ecosystem-triggered transfers
  • Network: Base (Ethereum L2)
  • Token Standard: ERC-20 with burn extensions

Tokenomics

Initial Circulation: 100% at launch

Burn Mechanism

Trigger: Ecosystem address transfers

Effect: Permanent supply reduction

Destination: Burn address (0x0)
