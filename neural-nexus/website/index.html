The $NAE Burn Engine

01. Genesis Burn

We started with fire. 25% of the total $NAE supply was sent to a dead address at launch, creating immediate and permanent scarcity.

02. Ecosystem Tax

A 25% fee is applied to all transfers involving ecosystem addresses (dApps, specialized wallets, and launchpads).

03. 100% Burn

Every single wei of the transaction fee is immediately and automatically burned. No redistribution, just removal.

04. Finite Supply

No new $NAE can ever be minted. The supply only goes one way: Down.
