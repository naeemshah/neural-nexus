- **Colors:** Emerald Cyber (#00FF9F), Dark Cyber (#0B0E14)
- **Typography:** Clean, modern, tech-focused
- **Graphics:** Futuristic, data-driven, minimal

2. **Infographics:** Data visualization of tokenomics, roadmap
3. **Video Content:** Short explainers, team introductions
4. **Interactive:** Polls, quizzes, AMAs

- **Professional:** Technically accurate, well-researched

## Crisis Management Plan

### Potential Issues
1. **FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt):** Prepared responses, transparency
2. **Technical issues:** Clear communication, regular updates
3. **Market volatility:** Focus on fundamentals, long-term vision
4. **Community conflicts:** Clear moderation, conflict resolution

### Communication Protocol
1. Acknowledge issue immediately
2. Gather facts and assess impact
3. Prepare clear, transparent response
4. Communicate through all channels
5. Follow up with resolution
