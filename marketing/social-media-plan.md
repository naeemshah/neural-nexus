## Risk Management

### Content Risks:
- **Misinformation:** Clear communication, fact-checking
- **Overpromising:** Realistic roadmap, transparent updates
- **Regulatory compliance:** Legal review of all public statements

### Community Risks:
- **Spam/bots:** Strong moderation, verification systems
- **FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt):** Transparent communication, quick response
- **Scams:** Official channel verification, security education

### Platform Risks:
- **Account suspension:** Follow platform rules, backup communication channels
- **Algorithm changes:** Diversify content types, engage authentically
